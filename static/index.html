Instructions & Tips

About This App

This application uses advanced AI to identify plant diseases from leaf images. It can help you:

  • Quickly identify common plant diseases
  • Get detailed information about the detected disease
  • Learn about symptoms and treatment options

For Best Results

To get the most accurate results, please ensure your image meets these requirements:

  • Image Quality: Use a clear, well-lit photo
  • Focus: Capture a single leaf (preferred) or a small group of affected leaves
  • Resolution: Higher resolution images work better
  • Background: Try to have a neutral or contrasting background
  • Angle: Take the photo from directly above the leaf

Supported Plants

Currently, the app can identify diseases in:

  • Tomato plants
  • Potato plants
  • Pepper plants
